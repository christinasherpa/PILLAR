When users open the app, they are directed to the log in page.
They can log in with their email and password.
In the case that the user is new to our app,
they have the option of signing up for a new account.
Rings are one of the features of Pillar.
Users can join and create Rings.
This keeps the users' feed more organized
since Rings categorizes posts by topic.
When users have free time,
they can browse through recent posts and
help someone else on the Explore page.
Users can access their posts,
courtesy coins count, and account settings
from their Profile page. They can also
request to change their password or
email through account settings.
